What About Us
Your Trusted Partner in Mobile Healthcare

Daktari Africa provides easy, mobile access to medical care for everyone as a trusted partner.

We connect people with medical needs to doctors and other clinicians with solutions by using a dependable, efficient, mobile platform. That way, patients on follow-up care, both urban and remote, and even some emergencies, can receive quick, quality, affordable medical help at the touch of a button.

Our service-oriented website and mobile app enable clinical consultations and provide medical information to help you take better control of your health.

Why Choose Daktari Africa?
  • Looking for a medical specialist? Find their contact here
  • Need a doctor from your insurance panel? We've got you covered
  • Traveling and need a doctor in a new county? We connect you locally
  • Award-winning platform - Vision 2030 recognition by Kenya ICT Board
  • Founded by experienced medical specialists with world-class IT experts
  • Quick, quality, affordable medical help at the touch of a button

Vision 2030 Award: Recognized by the Kenya ICT Board in 2012 for innovative healthcare solutions.
